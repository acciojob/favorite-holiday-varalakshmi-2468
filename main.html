Paris, France


Paris, the City of Light, is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the world. Known for its rich culture, stunning architecture, and world-famous landmarks, Paris offers an unforgettable holiday experience for travelers of all ages.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous avenue for shopping and entertainment.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A romantic way to see Paris at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky and buttery French pastries.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate French sweets.
  • Escargot – Traditional French dish of snails.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Cheese – Rich and flavorful varieties to try.
